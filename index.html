About the Game


Command your Light Cruiser in real-time space warfare, using physics, advanced battle tactics, or surprise your enemies by ambushing them from a stealthy position.

Traverse the Neptunian system collecting all the resources you need to repair your aging vessel, and rebuild what humanity has left. Guerilla tactics will be your ally in this shooting war, but when all your plans fall apart you must above all, survive contact with the enemy.

Starlight brigade will be available for $14.99 on Steam. With builds for both Windows and Linux with a release date of: December 2023
