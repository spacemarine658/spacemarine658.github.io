Starlight Brigade Press Kit

Starlight Brigade is a tactical space combat game designed by J Newhart (AKA Spacemarine658) for sale on Steam.

Quick Facts

Developer: J Newhart
Location: Texas
Release date: December 2023
Platform: Steam
Price: $14.99

What is Starlight Brigade

Command your Light Cruiser in real-time space warfare, using physics, advanced battle tactics, or surprise your enemies by ambushing them from a stealthy position. Traverse entire solar systems collecting all the resources you need to rebuild humanity on a new homeworld. But when your plan falls apart you must above all, survive contact with the enemy.
